What Is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that lets you create professional videos using realistic AI avatars — no cameras, actors, or studios required. Founded in 2017 by a team of AI researchers from UCL and Stanford, it's now used by over 50,000 companies including Reuters, Amazon, Accenture, and Nike.

The core concept is simple: you type a script, choose an AI avatar, and Synthesia generates a video of that avatar speaking your words with natural lip-syncing, facial expressions, and body language. It supports over 140 languages, making it a powerful tool for global teams.

Unlike general-purpose AI video tools that generate artistic or cinematic content, Synthesia is laser-focused on business video — employee training, product explainers, customer onboarding, sales enablement, and internal communications. That focus is both its greatest strength and its biggest limitation.

Key Features

1. AI Avatars (140+ Options)

Synthesia's avatar library is one of the most diverse in the industry. You get access to over 140 stock avatars across different ethnicities, ages, genders, and professional styles. Each avatar features realistic lip-syncing, natural eye movement, and subtle facial expressions that make the output look genuinely human.

For enterprise users, you can create a custom AI avatar — a digital twin of yourself or your team members. This requires recording about 15 minutes of footage, and Synthesia's AI builds a personalized avatar you can use indefinitely. It's surprisingly accurate and a game-changer for teams that want a consistent on-camera presence without actually being on camera.

2. Text-to-Video Editor

The editor works like a PowerPoint-meets-script-writer interface. You type your script in a text panel, add scene breaks, insert slides or images, and the AI generates video for each section. The workflow is intuitive — if you can write a document, you can create a video.

Key editor features include:

  • Scene-based editing: Break your video into scenes, each with its own avatar position and background
  • Media library: Stock images, videos, icons, and shapes built in
  • Screen recording: Record your screen and combine it with AI avatar narration
  • Branded templates: Save your brand colors, logos, and layouts for consistent output
  • Gesture control: Make avatars point, nod, or raise their eyebrows at specific moments

3. 140+ Languages & Auto-Translation

This is where Synthesia truly shines. Create a video in English, then automatically translate the entire script into 140+ languages with one click. The AI avatar's lip movements adjust to match the new language — it's not perfect, but it's remarkably close.

For global companies running training programs across multiple countries, this feature alone can save tens of thousands of dollars in translation and re-shooting costs. You create one master video and generate localized versions for every market in minutes.

4. AI Script Assistant

Synthesia includes a built-in AI scriptwriter powered by GPT. You can enter a topic, paste a document, or provide a URL, and the AI generates a polished video script. You can then edit, refine, and convert it directly to video. It's helpful for getting started quickly, though the output usually needs some human refinement.

5. AI Video Templates

The platform offers 200+ professionally designed templates for common use cases:

  • Employee onboarding & orientation
  • Compliance & safety training
  • Product demos & explainers
  • Sales enablement & pitch decks
  • Customer support & FAQ videos
  • Internal communications & announcements

Templates include pre-written scripts, avatar positioning, backgrounds, and transitions — just customize the content and generate.

6. SOC 2 Compliant & Enterprise-Ready

Synthesia takes security seriously. The platform is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and offers SSO, role-based access controls, and custom data processing agreements. For enterprise teams handling sensitive training content (healthcare, finance, government), this level of compliance is essential.

Synthesia vs. Competitors

Synthesia vs. HeyGen

HeyGen is Synthesia's most direct competitor, offering similar AI avatar video generation with a stronger focus on marketing and social media content. HeyGen provides more casual, creative avatar styles and is generally cheaper ($24/month for 15 minutes vs. Synthesia's $18/month for 10 minutes).

Synthesia wins on enterprise features, compliance (SOC 2), avatar quality for professional contexts, and its deep template library for corporate training. HeyGen wins on creative flexibility, social media templates, voice cloning quality, and price-to-minutes ratio.

Verdict: For corporate training, onboarding, and enterprise video → Synthesia. For marketing, social media, and creative content → HeyGen.

Synthesia vs. Runway ML

These tools serve fundamentally different purposes. Runway ML is a creative AI video platform focused on generative video, cinematic effects, and artistic content. Synthesia is a business video platform focused on avatar-driven communication.

If you need to create a training video for 5,000 employees in 12 languages, Synthesia is the clear choice. If you need to generate a cinematic brand video with AI art, Runway ML is what you want. They complement each other rather than compete directly.

Synthesia vs. InVideo AI

InVideo AI focuses on text-to-video creation using stock footage, animations, and templates — similar to a Canva for video. Synthesia uses AI avatars as the primary content medium. InVideo is better for social media clips and marketing videos with stock footage. Synthesia is better for any scenario where you need a "person" on screen presenting content.

Pros & Cons

  • Intuitive editor — if you can write a script, you can create a video; zero video production experience needed

❌ Cons

  • Limited creative range — not suitable for cinematic, artistic, or entertainment video content

  • Custom avatars are expensive — creating a digital twin is an Enterprise-only feature with additional cost

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Synthesia free to use?

Yes, Synthesia offers a free plan that lets you create one test video (up to ~3 minutes) with limited avatars and a watermark. It's a great way to evaluate the platform quality before committing to a paid plan. No credit card required to sign up.

How realistic are Synthesia's AI avatars?

Synthesia's avatars are among the most realistic on the market. They feature natural lip-syncing in 140+ languages, subtle facial expressions, eye blinking, and head movements. For short-to-medium videos (under 5 minutes), most viewers can't immediately tell they're AI-generated. Longer videos occasionally show repetitive patterns, but the overall quality is impressive for business use.

How many languages does Synthesia support?

Synthesia supports over 140 languages and accents, making it the most multilingual AI video platform available. You can create a video in any supported language or translate existing videos with one click. The avatar's lip movements automatically adjust to match the new language.

Can I use Synthesia videos for commercial purposes?

Yes, all videos created on paid Synthesia plans come with full commercial rights. You can use them for marketing campaigns, social media, client presentations, paid courses, and advertising. The free plan includes a watermark and is intended for testing only.

Synthesia vs HeyGen — which is better?

It depends on your use case. Synthesia excels at corporate training, compliance videos, and enterprise content with its polished avatars, SOC 2 compliance, and deep template library. HeyGen is better for marketing content, social media, and creative video with more casual avatar styles and better pricing for volume. For enterprise and training teams, Synthesia is the safer choice. For marketing teams and creators, HeyGen offers more creative flexibility.
